SPECTO STUDIO

Specto Studio promotes the values of innovation, and interdisciplinary and creative freedom by championing the development and production of superior-quality contents and ground-breaking formats. Encouraging collaboration between established artists and young creators, Specto Studio brings together a wide array of disciplines including directing, set design, costume design, and music composition.

Owing to these objectives and tenets, Specto Studio has earned the status of social enterprise. This is a title that identifies activities dedicated to the creation of value of general interest and with civic and social utility purposes.

As a non-profit company, all fundraising achieved through sponsorships, grants, or revenues are entirely reinvested into the development of forward-thinking projects and the visions of masterful artists whose purpose aligns with the Specto Studio’s ethos and mission.
