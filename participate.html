There are no spectators in Il Mestiere del Tempo.

FOR PRODUCERS:
Offer your audience a transformative theatre experience by being the first to develop and deliver a fully-immersive live-cinema production. The production’s agile approach to theatre size, orchestral arrangement, and interactive exhibition spaces enable you to scale the entire production ecosystem to your vision, budget, and theatre.

FOR SPONSORS:
Investing in Il Mestiere del Tempo is not simply an opportunity to fund this production as a bystander. It’s an opportunity to become part of a whirlwind artistic journey, empowering you to cement your vision and play an integral role in a groundbreaking project.
