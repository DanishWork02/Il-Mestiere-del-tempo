The Project

Breaking the boundaries of traditional theatre, Il Mestiere del Tempo marries the otherworldly qualities of big-screen cinema with the enduring romance of Italian opera, creating a one-of-a-kind stage performance that’s as avant-garde as it is timeless.

Inspired by the themes of Made in Italy and The Italian Bottega, the project features an agile, interactive ecosystem add-on that can be seamlessly customised for your theatre space.

THE SHOW

Performed and sung entirely in Italian, the story of Il Mestiere del Tempo is the ultimate tale of juxtaposition. Traditional craft and forward-thinking technology coalesce as Olivetti’s villa is transformed into an engineering lab. The narrative takes place beyond the borders of space and time, where old-world Italian aesthetics harmoniously collide with the clean, modern minimalism that represents the character of Time.

A full symphonic orchestra throughout the show supports the sheer monumentality of the production -- but this is nothing like a Broadway play.

The director made a carefully-considered stylistic decision to contrast the maximalism of the production with a pared-down cast of only nine actors, realistic acting, and no dance pieces. The result is a rich, layered experiential journey that’s as much a sensory whirlwind as it is a deeply touching story.

Il Mestiere del Tempo was developed with modularity in mind, allowing investors to dictate the size of the production. The production can accommodate three set design options, from intimate venues to sprawling multi-level theatres, and live orchestral possibilities ranging from a quartet to a 40-person symphonic orchestra.

THE INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE

Like the story and style of Il Mestiere del Tempo, the production also exists outside of space and time. Inspired by the themes of Made in Italy and The Italian Bottega, the creator has developed an agile, interactive ecosystem add-on that can be seamlessly customised for your theatre space.


In Italian, the term mestiere directly translates to “craft”, but the true meaning is one of obsession, tradition, identity, meticulous attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to innovation. It is the thread that runs from the Renaissance workshop of Leonardo to the futuristic factories of Olivetti.


The production brings the meaning of mestiere to life through an exhibition space that allows the audience to read the original letters from the characters in the show, discover work-in-progress stages of the costumes and pieces seen on stage and immerse themselves into the Italian landscape of the Olivetti Era. Simultaneously, an interactive live Bottega allows the audience to experience an evolution of the creative space, through reconstructed artisans’ workshops: from tailors, to craftsmen, to the forefront of modern technology.

Together, these create a sensory experience that simultaneously gives investors another avenue to showcase their sponsorships for the production.

Through a delicate balance of cutting-edge technology, intimate performance, and immersive environments, Il Mestiere del Tempo masterfully evolves the medium of theatre production. Marrying tradition and innovation, we create a brand new way of interactive storytelling that places the audience at the centre of the experience.
